Guide 07

Founder essentials

The founder sets the tone. These principles keep the team motivated and the product focused.

Choose the right idea

Avoid ideas that rely on internal knowledge you cannot share. Make the pitch simple and transparent so volunteers believe it can work.

Get ready to pitch

You will pitch often. If you cannot explain it in 30 seconds, simplify. Pitching helps you refine or pivot the idea early.

Do not overcomplicate the MVP

Part time volunteers are slower than full time teams. Ship something minimal to production first and then iterate.

Be involved

The founder is the leader. Nothing energizes a team more than direct attention to product details and the vision.

Pitch checklist

  • Problem is clear and painful.
  • Solution is easy to explain.
  • MVP scope fits a few months.
  • Vision is exciting for volunteers.
