Guide 06

Phase playbooks

Each phase has a clear goal, key actions, and a concrete outcome that unlocks the next step.

Setup phase

1 week

Founder: 3 to 5 hrs/wk | Coach: 5 to 8 hrs/wk

Goal: establish your startup's professional presence and working tools.

Key actions

  • Build a simple website with vision and call to action.
  • Create LinkedIn and social profiles.
  • Set up hiring, team comms, and dev tools.

Outcome

Branding and infrastructure ready for recruitment and collaboration.

Kick off phase

2 to 3 weeks

Founder: 5 to 10 hrs/wk | Coach: 8 to 10 hrs/wk

Goal: build the initial team and establish daily rhythm.

Key actions

  • Hire first volunteer developers and designers.
  • Set up repos, workflows, and onboarding docs.
  • Start daily standups and assign first tasks.

Outcome

Developers onboarded, daily meetings running, work started.

Proto dev phase

8 to 10 weeks

Founder: 8 to 10 hrs/wk | Coach: 5 to 8 hrs/wk

Goal: move from setup to building real features in a working environment.

Key actions

  • Focus on core product features and UX.
  • Continue hiring as the backlog grows.
  • Hold meetings every second day and run demos.

Outcome

Development environment running with visible product progress.

Product dev phase

8 to 10 weeks

Founder: 8 to 10 hrs/wk | Coach: 3 to 5 hrs/wk

Goal: deliver a stable product ready for early users and scale the workflow.

Key actions

  • Focus on quality, security, and production readiness.
  • Introduce CI/CD and production environment.
  • Share early preview demos with testers.

Outcome

Stable product in production ready for early users.

Launching phase

4 to 6 weeks

Founder: 5 to 10 hrs/wk | Coach: 1 to 3 hrs/wk

Goal: release the product, onboard first customers, and start promotion.

Key actions

  • Feature freeze and release plan.
  • Select first customers and service them closely.
  • Marketing and promotion to build momentum.

Outcome

Product live with early customers and public traction.
