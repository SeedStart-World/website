Guide 02

The founder led, coach guided model

This chapter explains how founders, coaches, and volunteers work together to build a real product without upfront funding.

Core principles

  • check_circle Volunteers contribute real skills as team members, not interns.
  • check_circle Founder provides vision and leadership; coach structures the process.
  • check_circle Work happens on a real product with an MVP in production as the goal.
  • check_circle Daily or regular meetings create accountability and momentum.
  • check_circle Outcome: a functioning MVP, team in place, and proof for investors.

How it works

Founder

Vision and leadership

Sets the direction, prioritizes features, and makes decisions.

Coach

Process and tools

Creates rhythm, tracks progress, and removes blockers.

Volunteers

Execution

Build, design, test, and deliver the MVP in production.

Operating rhythm

The model works when the team follows a predictable cadence: short standups, visible tasks, and weekly demos. Use SeedStart to keep this rhythm transparent for everyone.

Your responsibilities

  • Founder: vision, scope, and product decisions.
  • Coach: rituals, onboarding, and delivery flow.
  • Volunteers: execution, collaboration, and feedback.
