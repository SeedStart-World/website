Chapter 05

Roadmap overview

A five phase journey from setup to launch, designed for part time founders and volunteers.

Phases and duration

Setup
1 week

Branding, tools, and hiring setup.

Kick off
2 to 3 weeks

First volunteers onboarded and rituals live.

Proto dev
8 to 10 weeks

Core features and UX progress.

Product dev
8 to 10 weeks

Stability, quality, and production readiness.

Launch
4 to 6 weeks

Release, first customers, and promotion.

Time commitment per phase

Phase Founder Coach
Setup 3 to 5 hrs per week 5 to 8 hrs per week
Kick off 5 to 10 hrs per week 8 to 10 hrs per week
Proto dev 8 to 10 hrs per week 5 to 8 hrs per week
Product dev 8 to 10 hrs per week 3 to 5 hrs per week
Launch 5 to 10 hrs per week 1 to 3 hrs per week

Roadmap foundations

  • check_circle Minimal branding and online presence.
  • check_circle Clear ideation and a simple value proposition.
  • check_circle Social media and website ready for hiring.
  • check_circle Daily or every other day meetings and demos.

Total journey

The full guided journey is designed to take about five to six months. With a steady cadence, you can reach production without funding.
