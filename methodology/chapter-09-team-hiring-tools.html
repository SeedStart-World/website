Chapter 09

Team structure, hiring, tools, and support

Build a structure that scales, hire for commitment, and use the right tools to keep momentum.

Team structure

Start with a two tier team, then move to three tiers when commitment grows.

  • Founder (Product Owner): focuses on product and vision.
  • Leads (Seniors): run meetings, assign tasks, review results.
  • Contributors (Juniors): execute tasks and share daily results.

Motivation and incentives

  • Team leads: motivated by the idea and learning to build a startup. Consider equity or partnership after MVP.
  • Junior contributors: motivated by experience. Give clear tasks and let leads manage them.
  • Scrum Masters: enjoy rituals and momentum tracking.
  • Product Owners: translate vision into clear goals and specs.

Hiring tips

  • Prioritize candidates with more time over more skills.
  • Choose people who respond fast and schedule interviews quickly.
  • Avoid candidates with low availability or unclear motivation.

Ideal profiles

Students, recent graduates, visa restricted talent, and candidates seeking local experience often have the most availability and motivation.
