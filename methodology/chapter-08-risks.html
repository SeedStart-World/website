Chapter 08

Risks and constraints

Every volunteer driven startup has risks. Name them early and plan a response.

No interest in the idea

If the idea is hard to explain or lacks a modern angle, it may be difficult to recruit volunteers. Clarify the pitch and validate demand.

Founder interruption

Long breaks can stall momentum. Document decisions and keep a backup lead to maintain progress.

Regional differences

The model has been tested in Australia and New Zealand. Adjust recruitment and communication for your local environment.

Lack of technical skills

Non technical founders may struggle to guide early development. Use a technical lead or advisor and document specs carefully.

Risk response plan

  • Improve the pitch and run discovery interviews.
  • Create succession and documentation for breaks.
  • Recruit with local context in mind.
  • Bring in a technical mentor early.
