Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-10

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how SeedStart.Space (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website and when you use our related services. The SeedStart application is available at app.seedstart.space and may present additional privacy notices in the app.

2. Information we collect

  • check_circleWebsite usage: basic log data such as device type, pages visited, and approximate location (via IP) if collected by hosting or analytics providers.
  • check_circleCommunications: information you send us (for example via social links or messages).
  • check_circlePlatform account data (in the app): information you provide to create an account and manage your startup workspace.

3. How we use information

  • check_circleProvide and improve the website, methodology, and platform experience.
  • check_circleOperate, secure, and debug services.
  • check_circleCommunicate with you about the product, updates, or support.

4. Cookies and third-party resources

This website loads third-party resources (for example Tailwind CDN, Google Fonts, and Swiper). Those providers may receive technical information (such as your IP address and user agent) as part of delivering content. Depending on configuration, we or our hosting provider may also use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality, performance, or analytics.

5. Sharing

We may share information with service providers who help us run the website or platform (for example hosting and infrastructure providers) and when required to comply with law or protect rights and safety.

6. Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law or necessary for security and abuse prevention.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to help protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your choices

You may be able to control cookies through your browser settings. If you use the SeedStart application, you may be able to review or update certain profile information within the app.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact SeedStart via the SeedStart LinkedIn page or the founder profile.
